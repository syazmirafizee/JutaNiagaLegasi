Recruiter (Sales Agent)

Juta Niaga Legasi Group
Full-time Permanent In-person

About the Role

Juta Niaga Legasi Sdn Bhd is seeking an experienced Recruiter (Sales Agent) to lead our team in driving sales growth and strengthening our brand presence in the property market. If you enjoy mentoring people and driving results, this is for you.

Job Responsibilities

  • Train/coach and mentor the Sales Team on achieving the KPI’s on a daily, weekly or monthly basis.
  • Develop and execute effective talent sourcing strategies to build a strong candidate pipeline.
  • Assess candidate’s skills, industry knowledge and cultural fit to ensure alignment with organizational needs.
  • Collaborate closely with the Sales Department to understand workforce requirements.
  • Develop new sales methods and strategies that will lead to a strong value proposition to prospects.
  • Manage end-to-end recruitment sales agents.
  • Stay updated on market trends and competitor hiring practices to attract the best talent.

Requirements

  • Minimum 2 years of proven experience leading a team of sales and proven ability to hire high-performing commercial and sales talent.
  • Strong determination, resilience and passion for achieving hiring targets.
  • Excellent communication, networking and interpersonal skills.
  • Result-oriented mindset with the ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.

Why Join Our Team?

  • End-to-end recruitment exposure - Manage the entire recruitment process, from sourcing potential agents to onboarding and supporting the growth of active team members.
  • Industry access & field opportunities - Gain valuable exposure through networking within the industry, site visits and hands-on fieldwork.
  • Collaborative & supportive culture - Be part of an open and encouraging workplace that values teamwork, new ideas and continuous learning in the recruitment process.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Pay: RM2,500.00 - RM3,000.00 per month
  • Opportunities for promotion
  • Professional development

How to Apply: Interested candidates may apply by sending your resume to: hrintelektisdigital@gmail.com

LIVE Host

Intelektis Digital
Full-time Permanent In-person

About the Role

We’re looking for a confident and energetic LIVE Host to represent our brand during live streaming sessions. If you enjoy talking to people, selling products, and being on camera, this role is for you!

Responsibilities

  • Host live streaming sessions on platforms such as TikTok or other social media channels.
  • Present and explain products clearly and attractively.
  • Engage with viewers, answer questions, and encourage purchases.
  • Promote products, campaigns, and special deals during live sessions.
  • Work with the team to plan live content and improve sales performance.

Requirements

  • Confident, talkative, and comfortable speaking on camera.
  • Good communication skills (Bahasa Malaysia required; English is a plus).
  • Energetic, positive attitude, and willing to learn.
  • Sales-driven mindset.
  • Experience as a LIVE host or in sales is an advantage (training provided).

Compensation & Benefits

  • Basic salary: RM2,000.00 per month
  • Attractive commission based on performance
  • Additional leave & Maternity leave
  • Cell phone reimbursement
  • Training, professional development, and promotion opportunities

How to Apply: Interested candidates may apply by sending your resume and/or short introduction video to: hrintelektisdigital@gmail.com
